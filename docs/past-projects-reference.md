## Spring 2024 (8 Projects)

### 1. Compassionate Coders — Shelter Bed Demand Forecasting
- **University:** University of Toronto
- **Members:** Emily Nguyen, India Tory, Nida Copty, Tom Nguyen
- **Problem:** Forecasting shelter bed demand in Toronto's homeless support system
- **Solution:** ML model to predict demand for shelter beds and resources
- **Key stat:** "In Toronto, over 9,000 people are homeless each night"

### 2. Ecolux Solutions — Energy Poverty Management
- **University:** University of Western Ontario
- **Members:** Nicholas Stranges, Katie Stevens, Thomas Michalski, Lauren Rejano
- **Problem:** Helping Canadians experiencing utility poverty manage energy costs
- **Solution:** App tracking live energy usage with actionable reduction insights
- **Key stat:** "19% of Canadian households experience Utility Poverty"

### 3. Grand River Translation — ASL Translation
- **University:** University of Waterloo
- **Members:** Charlie Liu, Tanguy Dieudonné, Yuna Choi, Robbie Knowles
- **Problem:** Limited progress in sign language translation accessibility
- **Solution:** Real-time app translating American Sign Language to English text
- **Key stat:** Over 500,000 ASL speakers in North America

### 4. Green Machine — Crop Yield Optimization
- **University:** University of British Columbia
- **Members:** Clara Parks, Katrina Arajas, Ece Celik
- **Problem:** Helping farmers maximize crop yield while promoting resource efficiency
- **Solution:** AI system recommending crop types using agroclimate, soil data, and weather predictions

### 5. NoEEG — Anesthesia Depth Monitoring
- **University:** University of Waterloo
- **Members:** Sofiane Sifaoui, Abhinav Rajput, Ethan Wellenreiter, William Kim
- **Problem:** Monitoring anesthesia depth in operating rooms without EEG sensors
- **Solution:** Model predicting BIS scores using physiologic and clinical signals only

### 6. PGCL Roomies — Liver Transplant Mortality Prediction

- **Members:** Liam Berry, Ryan Li, Bo Kang, Alyssia Naran, Anjola Adewale

### 7. RadioCare — Automated Radiology Reports
- **Universities:** University of Toronto, Simon Fraser University
- **Members:** Adibvafa Fallahpour, Archita Srivastava, Mantaj Dhillon, Grace Liu
- **Problem:** Manual radiology interpretation causes delays and inconsistencies
- **Solution:** System generating detailed radiology reports and identifying diseases automatically

### 8. Squad of Innovation — Dangerous Driving Detection

## Spring 2023 (7 Projects)

### 1. FoodGPT — Personalized Recipe Recommendations
- **Universities:** Carleton University, University of Toronto
- **Problem:** University students waste ~50% of food purchases; need cooking confidence
- **Solution:** ML model for personalized recipe recommendations to reduce food waste

### 2. GMZ — Forest Fire Detection
- **University:** Simon Fraser University
- **Problem:** Forest fire detection and prevention
- **Solution:** Computer vision model for fire/non-fire classification and high-risk area prediction
- **ML technique:** Image segmentation for vegetation feature detection

### 3. Techtonics — Earthquake Classification
- **Universities:** University of Western Ontario, University of Waterloo, University of Alberta
- **Problem:** Faster earthquake detection to mitigate climate-related seismic activity
- **Solution:** CNN classifying seismic signals (earthquake vs. noise)
- **ML technique:** Convolutional Neural Network trained on 100,000+ seismic images

### 4. QMAGS — Student Feedback Summarization
- **University:** Carleton University
- **Problem:** Professors struggle processing end-of-term student feedback
- **Solution:** EduEval web app summarizing feedback with side-by-side positive/negative comparison

### 5. U-Integrity — AI Plagiarism Detection
- **University:** University of Toronto
- **Problem:** Detecting ChatGPT plagiarism in student assignments
- **Solution:** ML model comparing academic performance between AI and human writing
- **Key finding:** ChatGPT produces average essays, fewer extremes than humans (630 AI + 6,230 human essays)

### 6. Turing Titans — Radon Exposure Prediction
- **University:** University of Western Ontario
- **Problem:** Radon exposure detection (WHO: 189,000 annual deaths)
- **Solution:** Predictive model for radon concentration using homeowner-accessible variables
- **ML technique:** Regression modeling

### 7. Tri-nauts — Stroke Risk Prediction
- **University:** University of Waterloo
- **Problem:** Stroke prediction and prevention (leading disability cause globally)
- **Solution:** ML calculator predicting individual stroke risk
- **ML technique:** ML model trained on 430,000 US CDC BRFSS survey responses (~93% accuracy)

- **Most common domains:** Healthcare (7), social services (4), environment (5), accessibility/mental health (4)
- **Most represented universities:** U of T (6), UWaterloo (5), UWO (3), UBC (3), Carleton (3)
- **Cross-university teams:** At least 5 projects had members from multiple universities
- **Team sizes:** Range from 3 to 5 members, most commonly 4
- **Common ML techniques:** Random Forest, neural networks, CNNs, LLMs, regression, classification, computer vision
