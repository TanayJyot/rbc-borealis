## Selection Process Timeline

1. **Application submitted** → Team review by RBC Borealis
2. **Short-listed teams** → Informal chat with an RBC Borealis mentor
   - Covers: project details, programming experience, ML knowledge, program expectations
3. **March 16, 2026** → All teams notified of decision

## Weekly Schedule (During Program)

- 1-hour team meeting (virtual)
- 30-minute mentor meeting (virtual)
- Remaining ~8.5 hours: independent project work
- Must have **minimum 2 consecutive hours** available Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm EST

## Attendance Requirements

- **Welcome Day:** Mandatory for all team members
- **Presentation Day:** Mandatory in-person attendance if within 3-hour commute of RBC Borealis offices (Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal)
- **In-person office visit:** At least one required for students in Toronto, Vancouver, Waterloo, Montreal
